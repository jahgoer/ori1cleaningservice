Our story

A local service built on higher standards.

Ori1 Cleaning Service was created with a simple belief: professional cleaning should feel dependable, personal and easy. Every visit is shaped by clear communication, thoughtful service and visible attention to detail.

Based in Coventry and serving Birmingham and the wider West Midlands, we work with homeowners, tenants, landlords, hosts and businesses of every size.

Whether we are preparing a home for new occupants or maintaining a busy workplace, our goal stays the same — to create a space you feel proud to walk into.
